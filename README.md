Image Puzzle — Clean, Fast & Fun

This project is a simple, minimal, and satisfying image puzzle app where you pick an image, choose your difficulty, and solve the grid. Everything runs smoothly in the browser, and the whole experience feels fast and lightweight.

🧩 What the app does
The app lets you turn any picture into a sliding puzzle.
You pick an image → choose the grid size → and the puzzle instantly generates.
It supports:

Multiple difficulty levels
Grids from 3×3 up to 6×6
Clean UI, responsive layout
Works on both desktop & mobile
Fast shuffle and instant restart

It’s built to feel casual, fun, and slightly challenging — depending on the grid you choose.

🎮 How it feels to use
The 3×3 grid is quick and breezy.
4×4 is the sweet spot.
5×5 and 6×6? That’s where things get real.
The app doesn’t slow down, and every move updates instantly.
Perfect for small breaks, focus resets, or just testing your own patience.
